## What's Trending in Window Treatments for 2026

Window treatments have evolved far beyond basic blinds. Today's homeowners are looking for solutions that combine style, technology, and sustainability. Here are the trends we're seeing across Metro Atlanta homes this year.

### Natural Materials Are Having a Moment

Woven wood shades, bamboo blinds, and linen Roman shades are increasingly popular among Atlanta homeowners who want to bring organic warmth into their spaces. These materials add texture and depth while complementing both modern and traditional interiors.

Natural fiber window treatments also offer practical benefits: they filter light beautifully, provide UV protection, and create a relaxed, inviting atmosphere that works perfectly in Georgia's climate.

### Bold Colors and Patterns

While neutral tones remain classic, we're seeing more homeowners embrace color in their window treatments. Deep navy shutters, emerald green Roman shades, and warm terracotta roller shades are all making appearances in Atlanta homes.

Patterned fabrics are also trending, particularly geometric prints and subtle botanical motifs that add visual interest without overwhelming a room.

### Smart Motorized Everything

Smart home integration isn't a luxury anymore — it's becoming a baseline expectation. We're installing more motorized shades than ever, with homeowners wanting voice control through Alexa, Google Home, and Apple HomeKit.

The biggest shift? Automated scheduling. Homeowners are setting their shades to open with sunrise and close at sunset, saving energy and maintaining privacy without lifting a finger.

### Layered Window Treatments

Combining different treatment types on the same window is a design trend that's here to stay. Popular combinations include sheer shades behind plantation shutters, or motorized roller shades layered with decorative drapes.

Layering gives you maximum flexibility: privacy when you need it, natural light when you want it, and a designer look that elevates any room.

### Energy Efficiency Drives Decisions

With Atlanta summers getting more intense, energy-efficient window treatments are a top priority. Cellular shades with honeycomb construction, insulated shutters, and reflective exterior shades all help reduce cooling costs.

Many of our clients are seeing measurable reductions in their energy bills after upgrading to modern, well-insulated window treatments — making the investment pay for itself over time.
